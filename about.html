The Beginning

Where Every Meal Feels Like Home

Born in the vibrant kitchens of Chennai, Hungry Hub was founded in 2015 with one simple goal — to serve soul-satisfying, authentic South Indian food that reminds you of home. From crispy dosas to aromatic biryanis, every dish is crafted with generations-old recipes and the freshest local ingredients.

Our head chef, Chef Murugesan, a native of Chettinad, brings over 20 years of culinary expertise. We also expanded our menu with crowd-favourite fast food — because great food should have no limits!
